Introduction

St. George Scout in Newcastle is a local chapter of the global Scouting movement, dedicated to nurturing leadership, teamwork, and outdoor skills in young people. Through our programs, we inspire Scouts to become active citizens, foster a love for nature, and develop skills that will benefit them throughout their lives.

We offer exciting activities, camps, community projects, and much more. Whether you're new to Scouting or looking to take your involvement further, St. George Scout is the place for adventure, learning, and growth.
