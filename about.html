What We Offer

At St. George Scout in Newcastle, we provide a wide range of Scouting activities designed to develop young people's confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills. Our weekly meetings, outdoor adventures, and service projects create a positive environment for growth and learning.

We offer exciting activities, camps, community projects, and much more. Whether you're new to Scouting or looking to take your involvement further, St. George Scout is the place for adventure, learning, and growth.
